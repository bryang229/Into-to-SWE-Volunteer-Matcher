Volunteer Matcher

Help Center

Find answers to common questions below.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the dropdown menu and fill out the registration form as a volunteer or company.

How do I apply for volunteer opportunities?

Once logged in, browse the available opportunities and click "Apply" on the ones you're interested in.

How do I update my profile information?

On your dashboard, click the "Edit" button next to each field to update your bio, location, age, and experience.
